Privacy Policy

At Liquid Clinical, your confidentiality and medical data security are our highest priorities. This Privacy Policy outlines our comprehensive protocols for securing, processing, and handling your personal health information, strictly fully compliant with international data protection protocols (GDPR & ISO-9001/2015 frameworks).

Patient Data Collection

We collect only the essential physiological, diagnostic, and personal data required to administer high-fidelity precision care. This includes digital health records, diagnostic imaging, and direct consultation logs processed exclusively on our encrypted, internal clinical networks.

Information Security Protocols

All patient data rests behind end-to-end multi-layered cryptographic encryption. Data retrieval is strictly limited to your designated medical specialists via biometric and multi-factor authentication networks.

Your Digital Rights

You hold absolute authority over your medical data. You may securely request, transfer, review, or permanently purge your historical records by contacting the Liquid Clinical Data Ethics team directly.
